CSR IMC Strategy — “Áo ấm cho em” (YODY)

Timed: Starting from February to the end of March 2025 • Master plan runs Feb 2 → Mar 2 (per report)

Report

Open Full Report (PDF) Download

Executive Company Snapshot (from report)

  • Revenue: grew 2019–2022 from ~70 billion to ~3,440 billion VND
  • Profit: not commensurate with revenue (report note)
  • Charter capital: increased to ~452 billion VND
  • Orientation: reach young customers, promote sustainable fashion, drive sales via direct shopping experience
  • CSR: allocate part of profit to “Warm clothes for children” fund (community impact)

SMART Objectives (overall)

  • Measurement (report): Awareness +12%, Liking +9%, Purchase +5%
  • Timed: February → end of March 2025
  • Behavior goals: raise awareness (new customers) + increase purchasing power (existing customers)

Big Idea / Key Messages / Hooks (from report)

  • Big idea: “Thời trang không chỉ là vẻ ngoài mà còn là tương lai” (Fashion is not only how you look, but also how your future is)
  • Key messages by phase:
    • Trigger: “Fashion brings along responsibility”
    • Engage: “Sustainable wear to make better life”
    • Amplify: “Sustainable loving”
  • Key hooks:
    • Advertising with model Tăng Thanh Hà
    • Talkshow at Vạn Hạnh Mall (Host: CEO YODY – Ms. Ngan Bui; MC: Thanh Thanh Huyen; Speaker: Chau Bui)
    • Minigame + Sales activity at stores

Master Plan (Trigger → Engage → Amplify)

Dates & phase objectives are taken from report Table (Trial + Objective).

Phase 1 — Trigger

2nd February – 14th February
  • Objective (phase):
    • Increase brand awareness by 4.65%
    • Increase brand liking by 1.0%
    • Increase purchase target by 0.8%
  • Key message: Fashion brings along responsibility
  • Supporting tactics (report):
    • Run ads: “Each customer who checks in at the talk show on February 15th will receive a blind box”.
    • On social media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
    • Billboard ad + at YODY stores
    • Sale products before the talk show
  • Channels: Social media + LED-screen billboard
  • Budget (phase): 376 million VND
  • KPIs (phase): 180,000 reaches • 31.1% brand awareness • 20% brand liking • 28.4% target purchase

Phase 2 — Engage

15th February
  • Objective (phase):
    • Increase brand awareness by 3.85%
    • Increase brand liking by 5.5%
    • Increase purchase target by 1.2%
  • Key message: Sustainable wear to make better life
  • Key activity (report): Talkshow at Vạn Hạnh Mall + livestream (Host/MC/Speaker as listed)
  • Supporting tactics (report):
    • Livestream talk show on social media (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
    • Check-in activity on Feb 15
    • Promotional program: vouchers, coupons
  • Channels: Banner + LED-screen billboard
  • Budget (phase): 165 million VND
  • KPIs (phase): 250,000 reaches • 40.5% brand awareness • 25.2% brand liking • 31.25% target purchase

Phase 3 — Amplify

16th February – 2nd March
  • Objective (phase):
    • Increase brand awareness by 3.5%
    • Increase brand liking by 2.5%
    • Increase purchase target by 2.6%
  • Key message: Sustainable loving
  • Supporting tactics (report):
    • Run ads at YODY stores and website
    • Post on social media (Facebook, TikTok, Instagram)
    • Sales activity at stores
  • Channels: Social media + YODY stores
  • Budget (phase): 80 million VND
  • KPIs (phase): 2000 products sold • 800 million VND revenue • 28.4% brand awareness • 29.8% brand liking • 40.35% target purchase

Effectiveness (KPIs) — How the plan is measured

This section mirrors the “effectiveness of the planned marketing communication program (KPIs)” in the report.

Phase 1 (Trigger) — KPI Execution Details

  • Target reach: 180,000 views
  • Assets: 3 videos (sustainable fashion themes) with participation of Tăng Thanh Hà
  • Media: 1 LED-screen billboard
  • Content: 15 posts on Facebook/Instagram/TikTok, including:
    • 5 educational posts about sustainable fashion
    • 3 interactive posts
    • 4 posts promoting YODY’s vision and new product collection
  • Expected outcome: brand awareness 31.1%, brand preference/liking 20%, purchase target 28.4%

Phase 2 (Engage)

  • Target reach/participants: 250,000 (attendees + external guests + livestream views)
  • Talkshow with fashion influencers → expected brand awareness 40.5%, brand liking 25.2%
  • Expected conversion: purchase target 31.25% to support the fund

Phase 3 (Amplify)

  • Goal: increase brand awareness 28.4%, popularity/liking 29.8%
  • Purchase target: 40.35% (compared to previous)
  • Sales goal: 800,000,000 VND revenue
  • Volume goal: ~2000 products, average price ~356,000 VND/product
  • CSR: contribute 5% of revenue to the fund (~40,000,000 VND)
