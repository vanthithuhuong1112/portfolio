IMC Promotional Plan — Tet Holiday in Ho Chi Minh City

Timeline: Jan–Feb 2025 • Framework: Trigger – Engage – Amplify

Overview

Promotional plan to attract domestic & international tourists to Ho Chi Minh City during Tet season, combining cultural experiences (festivals, food, landmarks) with digital + outdoor communication and influencer partnerships.

Open Full Report (PDF) See 3 Phases

Key Objectives

  • Increase tourists by 12.4% (Jan–Feb 2025 vs. same period 2024)
  • International visitors: +4.1%; Domestic visitors: +8.3%
  • Affective goals: positive sentiment +20%, preference +15%
  • Conative goal: +12% increase in travel-related purchase behaviors

Target Audiences

  • Domestic families (10–60): comfort/safety, family activities, culture + food experiences
  • Domestic couples & friend groups (18–40): events/festivals, travel vlogs, unique experiences
  • International couples & friend groups (18–40): interested in Tet culture; book via online platforms (Agoda/Booking/Grab/Traveloka)
  • Secondary audience: influencers/content creators to amplify reach and credibility

Creative Core

  • Theme: Happiness, Cherish and Memorable
  • Big idea: “Tết Việt – Vun đầy hạnh phúc, Kết nối yêu thương”
  • Key message: “Quây quần khoảnh khắc, đong đầy kỷ niệm”

Channels & Partners

  • Social (YouTube/Facebook/Instagram/TikTok) + livestream
  • Outdoor posters/billboards (near airport, malls, parks, hotels)
  • Travel platforms: Traveloka, Agoda, Booking.com
  • Influencers + press: vnexpress, tuoitre, Kenh14

3-Phase Plan

A structured funnel: Awareness → Engagement → Amplification.

Phase 1 — Trigger

9 Jan – 24 Jan
  • Raise awareness with short clips: “What does Tet mean to you?”
  • Collabs with celebrities + travel platforms
  • Hashtags: #TetMemories2025 #HappyTetinHoChiMinh #HappinessToCherish

Phase 2 — Engage

25 Jan – 2 Feb
  • On-ground experiences: flower festival workshop, Tet fair cooking
  • New Year’s Eve concert + fireworks at Bach Dang Wharf
  • Interactive “Magic Spring Forest” experience at Van Hanh Mall

Phase 3 — Amplify

3 Feb – 9 Feb
  • Recap content + influencer collaborations
  • Press coverage + thank-you posts
  • Push hashtag usage to sustain reach

KPIs (Planned)

  • Reach: ~1.25M; Engagement: ~1.3M (hashtag focus)
  • Hashtag usage: ~1.35M; Hashtag views: ~1.26M
  • Visitors: ~1.12M total; Staying visitors: ~700K
  • Media coverage target: up to ~84%

Budget Summary (VND)

  • Phase 1 (Trigger): ~1,536,000,000
  • Phase 2 (Engage): ~1,791,500,000
  • Phase 3 (Amplify): ~10,000,000
  • Total: ~3,337,500,000
